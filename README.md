# About Me

Hey, I'm Daumantas, 24, from Lithuania. Outside the game I am a programmer.

## WoW Background

I played World of Warcraft mostly during Shadowlands and early Dragonflight. During that time, I was highly motivated and constantly trying to improve, even though it was not always easy because I was playing on a low-end PC with low FPS.
Due to some life changes, I ended up taking a break from the game.

## Competitive Experience

Since then, my main game has been League of Legends, where I peaked at around 1.1k LP Master on EU.
After burning out on League, I recently came back to WoW and I can feel that same passion again. I'm excited to find a guild, start raiding, and keep improving.

## Playstyle

In general, I'm a very competitive person across all games, but I care a lot about playing for the team. I try not to be selfish, I'm willing to admit mistakes, take criticism well, and always look for ways to do better.
